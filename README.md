# Roles

## Town

Mayor
Abilities:
Accrue votes by abstaining from votes during the day. Each vote abstained is a vote gained.
Reveal yourself as Mayor during the day to use your accrued votes.
Attributes:
Your votes will appear as if you voted only once.
Goal:
Eliminate all evildoers and all who oppose the Town.
---
Sheriff
Abilities:
Decide between investigating and eliminating a player at night.
Attributes:
You can only attempt to eliminate players 3 times.
If the player you eliminate is part of the Town, you will be eliminated as well.
Goal:
Eliminate all evildoers and all who oppose the Town.
---
Deputy
Abilities:
Investigate a player at night for susipicious activity.
Attributes:
If the Sheriff is eliminated, you will become the new Sheriff.
Goal:
Eliminate all evildoers and all who oppose the Town.
---
Detective
Abilities:
Investigate a player at night to get a clue about their role.
Attributes:
None.
Goal:
Eliminate all evildoers and all who oppose the Town.
---
Medic
Abilities:
Visit a player at night to heal them. If they are attacked they will be saved.
Attributes:
Only your target will know if they were attacked.
Goal:
Eliminate all evildoers and all who oppose the Town.
---
Scout
Abilities:
Visit a player at night to witness who visits them.
Attributes:
If the player you visit is eliminated, the player who eliminated them will be aware that they were witnessed.
Goal:
Eliminate all evildoers and all who oppose the Town.
---
Veteran
Abilities:
Decide whether you will be alert at night.
Attributes:
You cannot be harmed while alert.
Anyone who visits you while you are alert will be eliminated.
You can only be alert 3 times.
Goal:
Eliminate all evildoers and all who oppose the Town.
---
Deflector
Abilities:
Deflect the action of a player at night onto another player.
Attributes:
You can only deflect targetable actions.
Your target will know their action has been deflected.
Goal:
Eliminate all evildoers and all who oppose the Town.
---
Swapper
Abilities:
Swap two players at night.
Attributes:
All actions targeting the two players will be swapped.
Goal:
Eliminate all evildoers and all who oppose the Town.

## Cult

Cult Leader
Abilities:
Decide between initiating a player and eliminating a player each night.
Attributes:
If there is an Initiate, you may not initiate a new player.
If there is an Acolyte, they will eliminate in your place.
Goal:
Indoctrinate or eliminate all who do not follow the Cult.
---
Acolyte
Abilities:
Eliminate a player each night.
Attributes:
You can decide who to eliminate if the cult leader does not decide for you.
If the Cult Leader is eliminated, you will become the new Cult Leader.
Goal:
Indoctrinate or eliminate all who do not follow the Cult.
---
Initiate
Abilities:
None.
Attributes:
If the Acolyte is eliminated or becomes the Cult Leader, you will become the new Acolyte.
Goal:
Indoctrinate or eliminate all who do not follow the Cult.
---
Manipulator
Abilities:
Manipulate two players at night to act on each other.
Attributes:
You can only manipulate targetable actions.
Your target will know their action has been manipulated.
Goal:
Indoctrinate or eliminate all who do not follow the Cult.
---
Fall Guy
Abilities:
Attempt to take the place of one of your fellow cult members at night.
Attributes:
Any action done onto your selected fellow will be put onto you anonymously.
Your action starts at a 50% success rate.
Each night you succeed it drops by 25% and each night you fail it increases by 25%.
Goal:
Indoctrinate or eliminate all who do not follow the Cult.

## Evil Individual
Sabotager
Abilities:
Trick the Town into voting out your target.
Attributes:
Your target is always a Town member.
If your target is eliminated at night you will become a Jester.
Goal: 
Get your target voted out at any cost.
